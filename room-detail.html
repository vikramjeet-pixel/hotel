About This Room

A Tudor Sanctuary

Step into a world where centuries of history meet contemporary luxury. Our Deluxe Room is beautifully appointed with an original four-poster bed draped in the finest Egyptian cotton, exposed oak beams dating to 1642, and a working stone fireplace that casts a warm, flickering glow on winter evenings.

Mullioned windows frame breathtaking views of the walled kitchen garden and rolling Warwickshire parkland beyond. The en-suite bathroom features a freestanding roll-top bath, a separate rainfall shower, and bespoke toiletries from our in-house spa collection.

Every detail has been considered — from the hand-stitched cushions to the curated selection of local books and artwork. This is not simply a room; it is an experience that will remain with you long after your stay.
