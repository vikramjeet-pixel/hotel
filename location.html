Getting Here

Travel Directions

By Car

Just 3 miles from Stratford-upon-Avon and 5 minutes from M40 Junction 15. Follow signs to Welford-on-Avon, then Kings Court Lane. Sat-nav: CV37 8EX.

By Train

Stratford-upon-Avon station is 3 miles away with regular services from London Marylebone (2 hrs). Warwick Parkway is 8 miles away. Taxis available at both stations.

By Air

Birmingham Airport is 22 miles (35 mins). London Heathrow is 85 miles (1.5 hrs). Airport transfers can be arranged through our concierge.

By Bus

The X18 bus runs between Stratford and Welford-on-Avon hourly. The nearest stop is a 10-minute walk from the hotel. We offer complimentary pick-up by arrangement.
